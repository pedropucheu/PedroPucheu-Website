Our Story

Fighting Productions was founded by Pedro Pucheu and Andrew Cleminson with a shared passion for combat sports and visual storytelling. We saw an opportunity to bring cinematic quality to an industry often underserved by professional content creation.

Our mission is to help fighters, gyms, and brands tell their stories in a way that resonates with audiences and drives real results. We believe that powerful storytelling creates a lasting connection between our clients and their target audience.
