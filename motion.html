Yan Texeira

This documentary profiles Yan Texeira, an MMA fighter training in Rocinha, one of Rio de Janeiro's most prominent favelas. The film aims to shine a light on Yan's lifestyle, and to reveal such difficulties to a wider audience.

The documentary examines systemic barriers faced by athletes in impoverished communities. Specific challenges highlighted include inability to afford recovery treatments, medical care, proper nutrition, and the simultaneous burden of family financial support.

I'm extremely grateful to have had the privilege to gain access to Yan's everyday life. Rather than focusing solely on sports achievement, this project functions as social documentation, revealing the intersection of athletic aspiration and socioeconomic constraint within Rio's favela communities.

A Celebration of Life

A short film exploring grief and loss through the perspective of a young protagonist confronting his mother's death. The narrative follows a boy who struggles with the memories of the recent loss of his mother which haunt him, and now attempts to overcome them.

The film emphasizes shifting focus from suffering to remembrance. Individuals process loss differently—through therapy, family support, or personal coping mechanisms. The core message advocates moving beyond dwelling on the suffering that our loved ones may have experienced before their passing to instead celebrating their lives and cherishing shared moments.

An Unknown Reality

This short film explores psychological themes through the lens of a protagonist struggling to distinguish between hallucination and reality. The narrative centers on a boy with a history of hallucinations who encounters a new entity he believes is genuinely real and poses danger to himself and others.

As he attempts to convince those around him, his therapist counters his perspective, suggesting the experience exists only in his mind. The film examines unreliable perception, mental health advocacy versus skepticism, and the tension between subjective experience and external validation.

This was my inaugural short film, created approximately one year after acquiring my first camera. The experience deepened my understanding of filmmaking's comprehensive nature: the importance of the harmony between all aspects of it—sound design, cinematography, acting...
