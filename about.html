About

Pedro Pucheu is a London-based filmmaker and photographer with a cinematic eye for authentic storytelling. His work spans commercial brand films, narrative shorts, documentaries and editorial photography.

From combat sports to fashion, food culture to community projects, Pedro collaborates closely with brands and artists to shape projects from concept through to final delivery. His approach is grounded — built on trust, preparation and a deep respect for the people and stories in front of the camera.

Whether it's a brand film, a wedding, a documentary portrait or a photography commission, the goal is always the same: to create something honest, visually compelling and worth watching twice.

What I offer

Video Production

End-to-end video production for brands and creative projects. From concept development and scripting through filming, editing and colour grading. Commercials, brand films, corporate content and wedding cinematography — delivered with a cinematic sensibility.

Photography

Portrait, editorial and event photography with a focus on natural light, character and mood. Available for campaigns, personal branding, press and live events across London and beyond.

Narrative & Documentary

Short films and documentaries that explore real stories and real people. Character-driven work with attention to craft, pacing and emotional resonance — from intimate portraits to observational pieces.
